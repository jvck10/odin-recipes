Pumpkin Soup Recipe

Description

This is Australia's #1 most-cooked soup recipe of all time and taste.com.au members are raving about it. Find out why!

Ingredients

  • 2T olive oil
  • 1 onion, finly chopped
  • 1 leek, white part only, finely sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/2t ground coriander
  • 1t ground cumin
  • 1/2t freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1kg peeled pumpkin, diced
  • 1 large potato, peeled, diced
  • 1L Massel chicken style liquid stock or vegetable liquid stock
  • 1/2C thin cream

Method/Steps

  1. Heat oil in large saucepan over low heat, add onion and leek and cook for 2-3 minutes, until softened but not coloured. Add garlic and spices and cook, stirring, for 30 seconds. Add pumpkin, potato and stock and bring to the boil. Turn heat to low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. Allow to cool slightly, then blend in batches.
  2. Return soup to pan, stir through cream and reheat gently. Season and add a little more nutmeg if desired.
  3. Enjoy.
